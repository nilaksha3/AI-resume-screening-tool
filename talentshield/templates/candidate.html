Career Tips

Keep Your Resume Updated
Regularly update your resume to reflect your latest achievements and skills. Tailor it for each job application to highlight relevant experience.
Prepare for Common Interview Questions
Research common interview questions for your field and practice your responses. Use the STAR method (Situation, Task, Action, Result) for behavioral questions.
Build Your Professional Network
Connect with professionals in your industry through LinkedIn and industry events. Don't underestimate the power of networking for finding job opportunities.
